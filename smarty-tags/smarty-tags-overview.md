## Smarty Tags

Modules can register Smarty tags that content editors use in page templates and content blocks. This is how modules expose their frontend functionality — a content editor places a tag like `{Holidays}` or `{cms_module module=Holidays}` in a page, and your module renders the output.

### In This Chapter

- [Plugin Tags](/modules/smarty-tags/plugin-tags/) — Register your module as a Smarty tag and create additional custom Smarty plugins.
- [Tag Parameters](/modules/smarty-tags/tag-parameters/) — Define, register, and document the parameters your tags accept.
